FitLife Wellness Hub - Site Plan

Site Name

FitLife Wellness Hub

Domain: fitlife-wellness.com

Reasoning: The name "FitLife Wellness Hub" was chosen because it clearly communicates the site's focus on fitness and overall wellness. The word "Hub" suggests it's a central place where people can find comprehensive resources, connect with others, and access various wellness services. The name is memorable, easy to pronounce, and directly relates to health and fitness, making it perfect for our target audience seeking a healthier lifestyle.

Site Purpose

FitLife Wellness Hub serves as a comprehensive online platform dedicated to promoting health and fitness through accessible resources and community support. The site provides:

  • Workout Programs: Structured fitness routines for different skill levels and goals
  • Nutrition Guidance: Meal planning tools, healthy recipes, and dietary advice
  • Progress Tracking: Personal fitness journals and goal-setting tools
  • Community Support: Forums and groups where users can share experiences and motivation
  • Expert Resources: Articles, videos, and tips from certified fitness professionals and nutritionists

Our mission is to make wellness accessible to everyone by providing reliable information, practical tools, and a supportive community that empowers individuals to achieve their health and fitness goals.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: The Busy Professional

Question: "I work 50+ hours a week and barely have time to cook, let alone exercise. What quick, effective workout routines can I do at home in 20-30 minutes, and are there any meal prep strategies that won't take up my entire weekend?"

Scenario 2: The Fitness Beginner

Question: "I've never been to a gym and feel intimidated by all the equipment and routines I see online. Where do I start as a complete beginner, and how do I know if I'm doing exercises correctly without injuring myself?"

Scenario 3: The Weight Loss Journey

Question: "I've tried multiple diets and exercise programs but always give up after a few weeks. How can I stay motivated and track my progress effectively, and what realistic goals should I set for sustainable weight loss?"

Color Usage:

  • Ocean Blue (#2E86AB): Primary color used for main headings, navigation elements, buttons, and key interactive elements. This color conveys trust, reliability, and calmness.
  • Deep Pink (#A23B72): Secondary color used for section headings, accent elements, hover states, and call-to-action highlights. This color adds energy and motivation.
  • Orange (#F18F01): Accent color used for important buttons, notifications, progress indicators, and special highlights. This color represents energy, enthusiasm, and achievement.
  • Supporting Colors: Dark gray (#333333) for body text, light gray (#666666) for secondary text, and light background (#F8F9FA) for sections.

Typography

Montserrat Bold - Main Headings

Usage: Site title, main page headings (H1), and important section titles

Characteristics: Modern, clean, and highly readable with strong visual impact

Montserrat Semi-Bold - Section Headings

Usage: Section headings (H2, H3), navigation menu items, and button text

Characteristics: Maintains consistency with main headings while being less dominant

Open Sans Regular - Body Text

Usage: All body text, descriptions, articles, and general content

Characteristics: Excellent readability, web-optimized, and pairs perfectly with Montserrat

Font Strategy: Using only two font families keeps the design clean and loads faster. Montserrat provides strong visual hierarchy for headings, while Open Sans ensures excellent readability for longer text content.
